Routechoices.com

Routechoices.com is an innovative live GPS tracking solution that is specifically designed for the sport of orienteering. The platform allows users to track the real-time positions of runners on a map, providing a unique and engaging experience for both participants and spectators.

To use the service, runners must carry a GPS tracking device, which can be a smartphone running a tracking app or a dedicated tracking device. This allows for accurate and up-to-the-second tracking of the runners' progress throughout the event.

Organizers can easily set up events through a user-friendly dashboard, which allows them to customize the event's settings, manage participants and trackers, and even use own maps. The platform also includes a frontend for listing events and displaying live and archived events, providing a convenient way for spectators to stay updated on the event's progress.

In addition to the frontend and dashboard, Routechoices.com also features a public API and a TCP server for listening to dedicated trackers, providing developers with the tools they need to create custom integrations and applications. The platform also includes a WMS server for serving event maps, allowing users to access the maps with a wide range of software.

Routechoices.com is a comprehensive and versatile solution for anyone looking to enhance the experience of orienteering events. Whether you're an organizer, participant, or spectator, this platform provides a unique and engaging way to track and follow the event's progress.

Rusty-Pullauta

Rusty-Pullauta is a powerful application that is designed to generate highly accurate maps out of LIDAR data input files. Built using the Rust programming language, Rusty-Pullauta is an efficient fork of the Kartta-pullauta windows software that takes advantage of Rust's performance to deliver faster and copy conform results on Linux, Mac and Windows.

With Rusty-Pullauta, users can expect to achieve up to 10 times faster results compared to the previous software. This is achieved through the use of Rust's ability to compile to efficient, low-level code without sacrificing safety.

Rusty-Pullauta supports a wide range of LIDAR data input file formats, including LAS, LAZ, and XYZ. The software also includes advanced algorithms for filtering, classification, and feature extraction, ensuring that users can generate highly accurate maps with ease.

In addition to its performance and accuracy, With its powerful features and fast results, Rusty-Pullauta is a must-have tool for anyone working with LIDAR data to generate orienteering maps.

Mapdump.com

Mapdump.com is a user-friendly platform that allows orienteering enthusiasts to store and share their maps along with their GPS routes. The service is designed to be simple and easy-to-use, making it accessible to users of all experience levels.

One of the key features of Mapdump.com is the included tool for calibrating any picture by selecting 4 points on a reference map. This allows users to overlay their GPS routes perfectly on the maps, providing a detailed and accurate representation of their orienteering activities.

Mapdump.com also offers an integration with Strava, which allows users to select their GPS activities directly from the popular fitness tracking platform. This makes it easy to import and share your orienteering routes with others.

In addition to its user-friendly interface and powerful features, Mapdump.com also offers a wide range of format for input and output. This means that users can upload and download maps and routes in a variety of different formats, making it easy to share and view them on a variety of different devices and platforms.

Mapdump.com makes it easy to store, share and view your orienteering maps along with your GPS routes. Its user-friendly interface, powerful features, and integration with Strava, along with wide range of format makes it the perfect tool for orienteering enthusiasts of all experience levels.

Verbicruciste.fr

Verbicruciste.fr is a website for French crossword puzzle enthusiasts and creators. It provides a user-friendly platform that makes it easy to build and share crossword puzzles with others.

The website is designed to be intuitive and simple to use, allowing users to quickly create a crossword puzzle grid, fill it in with words, and share it with their audience. Whether you're a seasoned crossword creator or a beginner, Verbicruciste.fr makes it easy to build and share your puzzles with others.

The website was originally built as a present for my mother, who is passionate about crossword puzzles. I wanted to create a platform that would allow her to easily create and share her puzzles with others, and that's exactly what Verbicruciste.fr does.

Verbicruciste.fr makes it easy to create, share and enjoy French crossword puzzles. With its user-friendly interface, customizable options and with its goal of being easy to use, it's a perfect tool for anyone looking to create and share crossword puzzles in French.
