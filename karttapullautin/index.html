Karttapullautin

The award winning* toolbox/workflow for generating orienteering training maps without field checking.

Download karttapullautin v2

Karttapullautin v2 runs on Windows, Mac and Linux.
It uses lots of memory and CPU.
Processing 6 km2 map takes about 1 minute with relatively new laptop.
With over 30..40 km2 you will most likely run out of memory, so any larger areas should be batch processed tile by tile.

Discussions, experiments and piles of maps made with Karttapullautin can be found here and here.

Karttapullautin v2 is free and open source.

*Maps4Finland award, 3rd in apps category and public votes winner in Apps4finland contest 2012 apps category.
